Front Street Cantina in Plainfield, IL serves fantastic authentic mexican cuisine that keeps my family coming back for more. Their chips and sala are always fresh and the portions are a good size but not huge. The staff is friendly and attentive and we are always seated right away, have never had to wait. The best dish I have had there is the chicken enchilada. They have great kid's meals and are very good about drink refills. Their lunch specials make for an economical way to have a nice lunch out.
Nevin's Brewing Company in Plainfield, IL serves a wide seletion of craft beers made by them and other local breweries. You can even take home some of the wonderful beer in a Growler! In addition, their menu is full of delicious lunch and dinner choices at very reasonable prices. My favorite is the spinach salad, which includes pears, walnuts, cranberries and blue cheese. Also their beer and cheddar soup is out of this world! They have weekly trivia nights and weekend bands with plenty of room to spread out.
